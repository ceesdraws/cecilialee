About

Born and raised in Argentina, Cecilia Lee is a children's book illustrator living in Brooklyn, New York. She moved to America in her early teens and earned a degree in Spanish Literature and Philosophy from New York University. Her drive to illustrate children's books was sparked by her time working on a permaculture farm in Bogota, Columbia. When she's not drawing poop, you can probably find her at a coffee shop drawing puns. See more of her work at ceesdraws
